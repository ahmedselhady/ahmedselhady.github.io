I am a Deep Learning Researcher and Engineer interested in Natural Language Processing and anything related to text and knowledge. <br/>
I worked as a NLP Research Engineer at [Agolo](https://www.agolo.com/) for three years under [Mohamed Al-Tantawy](https://www.linkedin.com/in/altantawy). After which, I joined Microsoft's Egypt Office working in the Content Moderation and Understanding team under [Motaz El-Saban](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=xHcqzWgAAAAJ&hl=en) and [Reza Ghaeini](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=2qulILsAAAAJ&hl=en). <br/>
I pursued my Master of Science Degree in Deep Learning at Zewail University of Science and Technology, working on improving factual accuracy in abstractive summarization, supervised by [Dr. Khaled Mostafa EL Sayed](https://scholar.google.com.eg/citations?user=cnfPK10AAAAJ&hl=en). <br/>
Recently, I have been accepted in University of Basque Country's Ph.D. Program, where I will join the Language and Analysis department under the supervision of [Dr. Eneko Agirre](https://eagirre.github.io/) and [Dr. Mikel Artetxe](https://www.mikelartetxe.com/).
