TL;DR: We introduce a guided continued pretraining stage for pretrained language models that improves understanding of factual attributes of clinical documents. We show the effectiveness of this approach in adapting general-purpose BART model to domain-specific dataset (MSLR - Multi-document Summarization of Literature Review).
